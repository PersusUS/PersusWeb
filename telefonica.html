Telefonica

Telefónica organized a visit to Sevilla as part of the InnovaTE initiative, a prominent technology exhibition designed to showcase and demonstrate the latest innovative solutions in the tech industry. This event provided a unique platform for attendees to engage with cutting-edge technologies, many of which had been highlighted at the renowned Mobile World Congress (MWC) held in Barcelona.
At InnovaTE, I had the opportunity to present in the sustainability section, where I showcased innovative approaches and solutions aimed at promoting environmental responsibility within the tech industry. This was an exciting chance to share my insights and contribute to discussions on how technology can play a pivotal role in achieving sustainable development goals.
Additionally, I had the honor of introducing the Mayor of Sevilla during the event. This moment underscored the importance of collaboration between local government and the tech community in fostering a sustainable future. The exhibition not only aimed to inform but also to inspire dialogue among industry professionals, startups, and the general public about the critical intersection of technology and sustainability.
Through this initiative, Telefónica sought to promote technological literacy and foster an environment where new ideas could flourish, ultimately paving the way for future advancements in the digital landscape.
