Netkey

Netkey is an innovative technology company that specializes in enhancing networking experiences through cutting-edge solutions. Our flagship product is a 3D-printed NFC (Near Field Communication) device designed to streamline and elevate networking activities. By seamlessly integrating with Linktree, our solution allows users to share their professional profiles, portfolios, and social media links effortlessly.
At Netkey, we believe in the power of connectivity. Our NFC-enabled devices are not only functional but also customizable, providing users with a unique way to present their personal brand. With a simple tap, users can transfer their information to smartphones, eliminating the need for traditional business cards and enabling a more sustainable approach to networking.
Our commitment to innovation drives us to continually improve and expand our product offerings, ensuring that we stay at the forefront of technology in the networking space. Whether you are at a conference, a business meeting, or a casual gathering, Netkey empowers you to connect, engage, and grow your professional network with ease and efficiency.
