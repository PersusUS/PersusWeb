AI Mouse

AI Mouse is an innovative artificial intelligence project that utilizes computer vision to simulate a mouse, allowing users to control their computers through the movement of their hands. This contactless approach provides an intuitive and ergonomic user experience, eliminating the need for a physical mouse.
With the capability to detect and track hand movements, AI Mouse translates gestures into mouse actions such as clicks and scrolling. This enables fluid and natural interactions, making it ideal for design applications, gaming, and presentations. Users can also customize specific gestures to execute commands and functions, enhancing efficiency and comfort.
AI Mouse is universally compatible with any device that supports mouse input, making it versatile across different operating systems and applications. The project's aim is to transform how we interact with computers, offering an accessible, innovative, and user-friendly alternative for everyday tasks and digital experiences.
